Mathematics: Difference of Two Squares

Difference of Two Squares

(a - b)(a + b) = a² + ab - ab - b² = a² - b² Hence, a² - b² = (a + b)(a - b)

Examples: Factorise the following

  1. y² - 4 = (y)² - (2)² = (y - 2)(y + 2)
  2. 36 - 9a² = 6² - (3a)² = (6 - 3a)(6 + 3a)
  3. Factorise: 5a² - 45 = 5(a² - 9) = 5[(a - 3)(a + 3)]
